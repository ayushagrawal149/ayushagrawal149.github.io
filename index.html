About

- Albert Szent-Gyorgyi

I am a colossal research & tech enthusiast and ever curious about everything going around me. My goal in life is to make people's lives better using my engineering and analytical skills. Research just happens to be my chosen path to it! Being an undergraduate, my research interests are not yet completely defined but I am fascinated by molecular simulations, mathematical modelling, polymers and machine learning.

Currently pursuing Bachelor's of Technology (B.Tech.) with Honors in Chemical Engineering at IIT Bombay, I dream to be Dr. Akshat Zalte someday.
